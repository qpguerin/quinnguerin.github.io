About

What I do

I'm a mechanical engineering student at the University of Iowa who likes problems with a physical answer — something you can model, print, fly, and (occasionally) rebuild after a hard landing. Most of my hands-on time goes into CAD work: modeling parts, checking fits, and iterating designs until they actually go together the way the drawing says they should.

My main personal project is building and flying FPV drones — designing and printing frames, tuning flight controllers, and learning a lot about vibration, weight, and durability the hard way. It's where CAD design meets real-world testing, which is the kind of engineering I want to keep doing.
